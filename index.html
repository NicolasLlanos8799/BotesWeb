Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your tour.

Yes. Every Seaduced Experience tour is 100% private, reserved only for your group. We operate year-round, and our tours are designed to offer a warm and cozy hygge experience even in the colder months.

Our tours typically last 1 to 2 hours, with options up to 4 hours. We explore central canals (Nyhavn, Christianshavn, Islands Brygge) and can include stops at Reffen or Trekroner Fort on longer trips. As everything is private, your captain can customize the route to your interests.

We provide blankets and hot drinks in winter, and cold water in summer. Some packages include snacks or champagne. You are welcome to bring your own food/drinks for trips of 3+ hours. We recommend comfortable clothing and layers for the weather.

Payments: We accept Credit/Debit cards (Visa, MC, AMEX), MobilePay, Apple Pay, SumUp, and Revolut.

Cancellations:

  • 7+ days before: Full refund.
  • 7 days to 48h before: 50% refund.
  • Within 48h: Non-refundable.

In case of bad weather/unsafe conditions, we reschedule or offer a full refund. Refunds take 5–10 business days.
