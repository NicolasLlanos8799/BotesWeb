In detail

Experience Copenhagen like never before: from the water, at your own pace. Discover the city’s most iconic sights on a private tender boat with your own atmosphere.

Join captain Oni on the elegant Joda Tender 710 for a journey through scenic canals. Hear local stories, discover hidden gems, and soak in the historical insight of the Danish capital.

Highlights

  • Custom Route: See the places that interest you most.
  • Swim Stops: Refresh yourself in the clean canal waters.
  • Iconic Sights: Perfect views of the Little Mermaid and more.
  • Street Food: Optional stop at Reffen market.
  • Vibe: Private, intimate, and fully at your own pace.

Included

  • 1, 3, or 4-hour boat tour in Copenhagen
  • Live commentary and guiding from captain Oni
  • Bluetooth speaker for your own music
  • Drinks available for purchase on board

Exclusivity & Vibe

Your trip, your vibe: connect your playlist, open a cold drink from the cooler, and cruise in style. This tour offers total flexibility for couples, families, or small groups.
