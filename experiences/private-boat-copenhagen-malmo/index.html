Copenhagen to Malmö Private Boat Experience

Discover two countries in one unforgettable day. Cruise from Copenhagen to Malmö on a private boat, explore iconic landmarks from the water, cross the Øresund Strait, and enjoy free time in Sweden — all at your own pace with your own captain.

Cancellation

Free up to 24h before

Participants

Private group (max 8)

Duration

7 hours

Guide

English, Spanish, Danish

In detail

Begin your journey in Copenhagen, cruising through the city’s vibrant harbor and passing some of its most iconic landmarks. From the colorful Nyhavn to the modern Opera House and the Black Diamond, enjoy a unique perspective of the Danish capital from the water.

As the city fades behind you, the experience transforms. You’ll cross the Øresund Strait, leaving the harbor for open sea. With music playing, drinks on board, and endless views of the horizon, this part of the journey becomes a true private escape.

Arriving in Malmö, Sweden, you’ll have around two hours of free time to explore at your own pace. Walk through the charming old town, enjoy lunch by the waterfront, or simply experience a second Scandinavian city.

After your visit, return to the boat and cruise back to Copenhagen, enjoying the calm rhythm of the sea and the relaxed atmosphere on board.

This is not just a boat tour — it’s a cross-border experience combining sightseeing, exploration, and open-water adventure.
