Floating Wine Tasting Experience

Duration 2h

Enjoy a unique 2-hour private boat experience in Copenhagen combining sightseeing with a guided wine tasting. Relax on the water with your own captain and host, taste four carefully selected wines, and enjoy a gourmet board while cruising through the city’s canals.

Cancellation

Free up to 24h before

Participants

Private group (max 6)

Duration

2 hours

Guide

English

In detail

Enjoy Copenhagen from the water on a 2-hour private canal cruise combined with a guided wine tasting experience.

While your captain navigates through the city’s most scenic canals and harbor areas, your onboard host will guide you through a curated tasting of four selected wines, explaining their origin, notes, and characteristics.

To complement the tasting, a gourmet board is served on board, including quality cheeses, cured meats, and fresh bread.

The atmosphere is relaxed, elegant, and intimate — ideal for couples, friends, small celebrations, or travelers looking for a premium experience beyond a traditional sightseeing tour.

This is a fully private experience for your group only, designed to combine great views, great wine, and a calm, exclusive setting on the water.
