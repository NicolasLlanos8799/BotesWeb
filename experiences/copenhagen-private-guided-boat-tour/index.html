Sail through Copenhagen on a private boat tour with your own guide

Descubre Copenhague como los locales en un paseo en barco privado al estilo «hygge». Relájate con música, bebidas y vistas panorámicas del puerto mientras descubres el moderno estilo de vida de la ciudad junto al agua.

In detail

Private guided boat tour in Copenhagen. Discover the city your way and experience Copenhagen like never before: from the water, at your own pace, and with your own atmosphere. Our private guided boat tours offer a unique and intimate way to see the city’s most iconic sights while giving you the freedom to shape the trip around your mood.

Step aboard the elegant Joda Tender 710 and meet your charismatic Cuban captain and guide, Oni, who will not only take you through Copenhagen’s scenic canals but also share local stories, hidden gems, and historical insight. Whether you want to relax with a drink, take a swim, grill some food, or dance to your favorite music, this tour is yours to shape.

What you’ll do

  • Stop the boat and enjoy a swim in the fresh water.
  • See the city’s top highlights from the water, including the Little Mermaid.
  • Customize your tour to see the places that interest you most.
  • Stop at Reffen, an urban street food market, for a bite.
  • Relax on the boat with a drink and enjoy the city skyline.
  • Fully private experience at your own pace.

What’s included

  • 1, 3, or 4-hour boat tour in Copenhagen
  • Live commentary and guiding from captain Oni
  • Bluetooth speaker for your own music
  • Drinks available for purchase on board

Exclusivity & Vibe

Your trip, your vibe: connect your playlist to the onboard Bluetooth speaker, open a cold drink from the cooler, and cruise in style. This tour offers total flexibility for couples, families, or small groups wanting something personal and easy.
