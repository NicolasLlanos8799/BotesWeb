Copenhagen: private guided boat tour through the city highlights

Sail through Copenhagen on a private boat tour with your own guide. Enjoy music, drinks, and stunning views, and stop for a swim, relax, or explore. It is your tour, your way.

General information

Free cancellation

Full refund if you cancel up to 24 hours in advance.

Reserve now, pay later

Flexible plans so you can secure your spot today without being charged now.

Duration

1 hour route focused on Copenhagen’s canal highlights.

Private group

Up to 8 guests with live guiding in English and Spanish.

What you’ll do

  • See Copenhagen’s standout waterfront landmarks from a private boat.
  • Enjoy your own music on board and shape the mood of the tour.
  • Pause to swim, relax, or explore when the moment feels right.
  • Pass areas like Islands Brygge, Nyhavn, Christianshavn, and the Opera House.
  • Take in the skyline, local stories, and a more intimate side of the city.
  • Keep the experience fully private for your group only.

In detail

This route is designed for guests who want the essence of Copenhagen from the water in a shorter, more flexible format. Your captain and guide, Oni, leads the experience with local insight, a relaxed atmosphere, and room to adapt the pace to your group.

Along the way, you can cruise past modern waterfront architecture, historic canal corners, and some of the city’s most photogenic views. With your own music, cold drinks on board, and a private setup, the tour feels closer to a curated experience than a standard sightseeing ride.

  • Private sailing through Copenhagen’s central highlights.
  • Bluetooth speaker on board for your playlist.
  • Flexible stop for a swim, a break, or a quick look around if conditions allow.
  • Ideal for couples, families, or small groups wanting something personal and easy.
