In detail

Private guided boat tour in Copenhagen. Discover the city your way and experience Copenhagen like never before: from the water, at your own pace, and with your own atmosphere. Our private guided boat tours offer a unique and intimate way to see the city’s most iconic sights while giving you the freedom to shape the trip around your mood.

Step aboard the elegant Joda Tender 710 and meet your charismatic Cuban captain and guide, Oni, who will not only take you through Copenhagen’s scenic canals but also share local stories, hidden gems, and historical insight. Whether you want to relax with a drink, take a swim, grill some food, or dance to your favorite music, this tour is yours to shape.

  • Your trip, your vibe: connect your playlist to the onboard Bluetooth speaker, open a cold drink from the cooler, and cruise in style.
  • City highlights tour - 1 hour: Islands Brygge, the Black Diamond, Slotsholmen, Nyhavn, Ofelia Beach, the Opera House, Christiania, Paper Island, and Christianshavn.
  • City tour + Reffen + the Little Mermaid - 3 hours: add a stop at Reffen and continue to one of Denmark’s most famous landmarks.
  • Full experience - 4 hours: includes the city, Reffen, the Little Mermaid, and Trekroner Fort, with panoramic open-water views.
  • Why choose a private boat tour? Exclusivity, flexibility, and a fully personalized experience for your group.

What’s included

  • 1, 3, or 4-hour boat tour in Copenhagen.
  • Live commentary and guiding from Cuban captain Oni.
  • Bluetooth speaker for your own music.
  • Drinks can be purchased on board: beer, wine, prosecco, champagne, and soft drinks. Bringing your own drinks is not allowed.
